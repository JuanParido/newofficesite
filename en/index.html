Welcome to the new office of Nokia in Sao Paulo! We will discover together a new environment of collaboration, productivity and motivation.

A workplace projected through concepts of modernity and orgainizational flexibility with a touch of innovation and comfort. Here, we want you to feel integrated with business and people of Nokia, feeling ready to evolve with us towards success!

Sinc our integration, we were waiting impatiently for the moment to be all together in a single place. Now, we are finally one only Nokia.

In this site, you will find all what you need to know about this new site: extensions, useful tips, office operation, site access, concepts of the corporate environment and virtual office, and even more, such as advantages of the location, E-Business Park, information of public transportation and more.
