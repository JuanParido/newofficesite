KNOW THE SPACES OF THE MODERN OFFICE CONCEPT

Nokia workplaces: choose where you will work according to what you need to do

  • Individual work? Use Open Space (workstations)
  • Need to just email or do something that will not take long? Use Drop-in
  • Making a break or an informal conversation? Use the Coffee Points
  • Need a little relaxation? Use the lounges and informal areas
  • Will you conduct or attend a formal meeting? Use Meeting Rooms with Polycom, Projectors / TVs, and Glass Walls for Writing
  • Conference call? Use a Phone booth, soundproof and telephone speaker

Other things you need to know

Clean Desk Policy - Do not leave on the table:

  • Papers (documents, contracts, etc)
  • Books, notebooks and diaries
  • Equipment (cell phones, tablets and notes)
  • Calendars
  • Portraits or photos
  • Any personal objects

Noise Policy - Good practices for office silence

  • Speakerphone should not be used under any circumstances
  • Always carry the phone with you, keeping the call tone down
  • Do not talk to people through the workstations. Go to them. Maybe it's a good opportunity for coffee!
  • If your conversation is extended, get a phone booth or go to an informal area.
